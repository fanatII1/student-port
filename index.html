Introduction

Multimedia content is a combination of various content like audio, text, pictures, video, and animations within a single shot presentation. That is different from the traditional mass media, like audio recordings and printed stuff. (Jawad, 2020) Using this definition we can deduce that multimedia content creation is the process of producing content that combines and used all types of media content to convey a message. This typically means making use of elements such as text, images, audio, video, animations, and interactive features to create an engaging user experience. Multimedia content creation can be created for a wide range of purposes, including education, entertainment, marketing, communication, and more.

Guidelines on digital media convergence technologies for digital content creation

1. Understand your audience: Tailor your content towards your target audience

2. Incorporate multi-platform content creation: Make use of crossplatform compatibility for easier access and usage

3. Make use of interactive features: Enhance user engagement by incorporating interactive features such as polls and surveys

4. Stay up to date with new technologies: As technology evolves, one needs to be awake of the technology emerging in the digital media convergence space.

5. Use geotargeting: use geotargeting to curate content based on consumers in your area. It helps you create more relevant messaging for your target audience (Mailchimp, n.d.)

6. Use influencer collaborations: influencers can help expand your reach and add credibility to your content.
